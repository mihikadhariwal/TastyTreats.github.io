Carrot & Coffee Cake

Prep time: 20mins Cooking Time: 35mins Cuisine: American

A super easy batter loaded with shredded carrots, coffee, and topped with a thick crumbly cinnamon streusel.

Ingredients

  • 2 large carrots
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • pinch of salt
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2 cups coffee powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Peel and grate the carrots.
  2. Whisk sugar, melted butter, and eggs. Stir in carrots. Add flour, coffee powder, baking soda, and cinnamon. Mix until just combined. The batter will be very thick.
  3. Mix ingredients for the streusel.
  4. Spread batter into a greased 9-inch round baking pan. Sprinkle with streusel. Bake for 30 minutes until the center is just set.

Equipment Required

Oven

Mixing Bowl

Baking Pan
