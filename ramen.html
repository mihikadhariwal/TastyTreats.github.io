Homemade Ramen

Prep time: 5mins Cooking Time: 20mins Cuisine: Asian-Inspired

Take the usual ramen up a notch with this quick homemade ramen. Fresh veggies and herbs make this extra delicious, healthy, and cozy!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 3 teaspoons grated ginger
  • 4 teaspoons grated garlic
  • 4 cups broth
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 ounce dried mushrooms
  • 2 packages instant ramen
  • 2 cup chopped kale
  • 1 cups shredded carrots

Instructions

  1. Heat the sesame oil in a large pan over medium low heat. Add the garlic and ginger; stir fry for 2 minutes or until soft and fragrant.
  2. Add the broth and the water. Bring to a simmer; add the mushrooms and simmer for 10 minutes or until the mushrooms have softened and the broth is flavorful.
  3. Add the instant noodles to the hot liquid and simmer for an additional 5 minutes or until the noodles have softened.
  4. Remove from heat, stir in the kale and carrots, and a soft-boiled egg (optional). Season with chili oil, hot sauce, sesame oil, and/or soy sauce and salt to taste.

Equipment Required

Saucepan

Large Pot

Cooking Tongs
