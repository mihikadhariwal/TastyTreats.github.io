Sesame Apricot Tofu

Prep time: 10mins Cooking Time: 30mins Cuisine: Asian-Inspired

Tofu pan-fried into crispiness and made saucy, sticky, and sweet into this Sesame Apricot Tofu. An simple, delicious, vegetarian dinner that's super versatile and easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 1 block of tofu
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/3 cup apricot preserves
  • 1 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1-2 tablespoons vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon each cumin, paprika, and onion powder
  • 1-2 cloves garlic, grated
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • cooked rice
  • toasted sesame oil to taste
  • chives and/or cilantro for topping
  • steamed beans

Instructions

  1. Cut the tofu block in half. Press the water out of the tofu by wrapping it in paper towels. Let it stay like that for a few minutes while you prep the sauce.
  2. Whisk the apricot preserves, soy sauce, vinegar, cumin, paprika, onion powder and garlic together to make the apricot sauce.
  3. Take each piece of tofu and gently pull it into small chunks with your hands. Place the chunks in a bowl. Toss with soy sauce and a teaspoon or two of olive oil; then sprinkle with cornstarch and give it a few gentle tosses to coat.
  4. In a nonstick pan over medium high heat, heat the olive oil and then add the cornstarched tofu. Leave it undisturbed for a few minutes on each side, letting it get really nice and brown and crispy.
  5. Finally, add the sauce to the tofu and remove from heat.
  6. Top with the green onions and/or cilantro, sesame seeds, and sesame oil. Serve with rice and green beans, and finish with more salt and black pepper to taste.

Equipment Required

Mixing Bowl

Chopping Board

Non-stick Pan
