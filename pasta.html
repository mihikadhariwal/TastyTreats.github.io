Garlic & Sun-Dried Tomato Pasta

Prep time: 5mins Cooking Time: 20mins Cuisine: American

Garlic noodles and tender sun-dried tomatoes in a creamy, luscious sauce with a heavy dusting of Parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces pasta
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup sun-dried tomatoes packed in oil, sliced or chopped
  • 2/3-3/4 cup heavy cream
  • up to 1/2 cup reserved pasta water
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • chopped chives or parsley
  • parmesan for serving

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta according to package directions.
  2. Heat a pan over medium heat. Use a little bit of the oil from the sun-dried tomatoes to saut√© the garlic and sun-dried tomatoes until soft and fragrant, 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add the cream; bring to a simmer. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Stir in the cooked pasta, adding the reserved pasta water as needed to help coat the noodles in the sauce. Finish with chives and Parmesan.

Equipment Required

Saucepan

Mixing Bowl
