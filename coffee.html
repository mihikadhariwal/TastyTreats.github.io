Vanilla Mint Iced Coffee

Prep time: 5mins Cooking Time: 15mins Cuisine: American

Creamy, subtly sweet, Vanilla Mint Iced Coffee on repeat! Smooth and lush thanks to the cold brew and oat milk, with a special blend of flavor from fresh mint and whole vanilla beans.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • fresh mint (about 1/4 to 1/2 cup mint leaves)
  • vanilla bean
  • 1/2 cup strong cold brew
  • 1 cup oatmilk, or milk of choice
  • 2-3 tablespoons mint syrup
  • a couple of ice cubes
  • cream (optional-to add at the end as desired)

Instructions

  1. Simple Syrup: Add the water, sugar, and mint to a small saucepan and bring to a low simmer for about 15-20 minutes, until the sugar has dissolved and the syrup tastes like mint. If using the vanilla bean, split it down the middle and scrape out the seeds; add both the seeds and pod to the saucepan for simmering. Store the simple syrup in the fridge â€“ you can even leave the mint leaves and/or vanilla bean in the jar to concentrate the flavor further.
  2. Iced Coffee: To make the iced coffees, just pour the cold brew, milk of choice, and as much simple syrup as you want into a glass with ice cubes.
