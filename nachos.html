Nachos and Cheese

Prep time: 15mins Cooking Time: 40mins Cuisine: Mexican-Inspired

Spicy Lentil Nachos with Three Cheese Sauce-you will not believe how good these are! Saucy filling with a velvety homemade cheese sauce. Vegetarian.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup uncooked lentils
  • 4 cups enchilada sauce
  • 3-4 cups broth or water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • chips
  • cilantro, avocado, jalapeno, and lime for topping
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 4 tablespoons flour
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Rinse the lentils. Bring the enchilada sauce and 2 cups broth to a low simmer. Add the lentils and cook, stirring occasionally, for 30-40 minutes or until everything is soft and most of the moisture has been absorbed. If things are getting too dry, add some of the extra broth to the pot while it's simmering. The sauce should eventually take on a thick gravy-like consistency.
  2. Meanwhile, melt the butter in a small saucepan. Stir in the flour.
  3. Add the milk slowly, whisking to smooth out any lumps of flour. Simmer until the mixture thickens. Remove from heat and stir in the cheese.
  4. Top the chips with a scoop of the lentil mixture, the cheese sauce, avocado, and cilantro.

Equipment Required

Saucepan

Grater
