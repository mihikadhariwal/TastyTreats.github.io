Homemade Tomato Soup

Prep time: 10mins Cooking Time: 50mins Cuisine: American

Homemade tomato soup with carrots, onions, garlic and tomatoes for deliciously rich flavor. It's super easy to make and is bound to be your next cold weather favourite!

Ingredients

  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • Half an onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Whole tomatoes
  • cream (optional-to add at the end as desired)
  • Rock Salt

Instructions

  1. Add carrots, onion, and garlic into a pan; saut√© for 5-10 minutes until the vegetables are very aromatic and tender.
  2. Add the tomato paste-stir for a few minutes until you can see/smell the tomato paste starting to brown and caramelize. Add the flour and stir for a minute or so.
  3. Pour the tomatoes and their juice into a bowl and crush them with your hands. Add the crushed tomatoes, broth, thyme, and bay leaf to the soup pot. Simmer for 30 minutes. Puree in batches and return to the pot. Add cream if you want. Season with salt.
